# User Story: 12 - Conduct Internal Audit

**As a** internal auditor,
**I want** to conduct internal audits of the AIMS,
**so that** the organization can verify conformity and identify weaknesses before external audit or certification.

## Acceptance Criteria

* Internal audits are planned at defined intervals.
* Audit scope and criteria consider relevant risks and management system processes.
* Objective evidence is collected and retained.
* Audit findings are reported to relevant management.

## Notes

* Based on Day 3 discussions around internal audit perspective, evidence, and using risk-related inputs during audit planning.
