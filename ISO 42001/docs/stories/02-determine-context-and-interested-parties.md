# User Story: 2 - Determine Context and Interested Parties

**As a** AIMS implementation lead,
**I want** to document the organization context and the requirements of relevant interested parties,
**so that** the AI management system reflects real business, regulatory, and stakeholder needs.

## Acceptance Criteria

* Internal and external issues relevant to the AI management system are documented.
* Relevant interested parties are identified.
* The requirements of those interested parties are captured and reviewed.
* The documented context is used as an input to planning and risk-related activities.

## Notes

* Based on repeated transcript emphasis that clause 4 drives later planning and that interested party requirements become management system requirements.
